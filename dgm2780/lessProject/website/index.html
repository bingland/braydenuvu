About Coolspa

Internationally renowned, multi-starred spa CoolSpa has opened a string of successful spas across the globe, from the UK and France to Singapore and Hong Kong, to the United States. Coolspa has also become a star of the small screen both in the UK and internationally, with shows such as Spa Wonderland, Idiots in Spas, Spa Balooza and Spantaneous US.
